Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: March 27, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

We respect your right to control your personal data. You may request deletion of all stored data associated with your account at any time, including Facebook Page tokens, YouTube credentials, and any account information stored on our servers.

This page explains how to submit a deletion request and what happens when one is received.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data via either of the following methods:

1
Send an Email Request Email us at support@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include the Facebook Page name or email address associated with your account so we can locate your data.
2
Disconnect from the Dashboard Log in to your account dashboard, navigate to Settings > Connected Accounts, and click "Disconnect" next to your Facebook or YouTube account. Disconnecting will immediately revoke the stored access tokens and remove your credentials from our system.

3. What Data Is Deleted

Upon receiving a valid deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Facebook Page access tokens
  • Facebook Page IDs linked to your account
  • YouTube channel connection tokens
  • Stored automation settings and preferences
  • Any account credentials or identifiers associated with your profile
Note: Published posts and videos on Facebook or YouTube are controlled by those platforms and are not deleted as part of this process. To remove published content, please use the respective platform's settings directly.

4. Processing Time

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt. All data will be permanently and irreversibly erased from our servers within that period.

5. Confirmation

Once your data has been fully deleted, you will receive a confirmation email to the address provided in your deletion request.
