1. Introduction

Welcome to our Video Automation Platform. This application allows users to connect their Facebook Pages and YouTube channels to automate the posting of AI-generated videos.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data. By using this platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the platform:

  • Facebook Page ID (to identify which page to post to)
  • Facebook Page access tokens (to authenticate and publish on your behalf)
  • YouTube channel connection tokens (to authenticate and upload videos)
  • User email address (if an account login system is enabled in the future)

We do not collect personal browsing data, device identifiers, or any information unrelated to the core functionality of this service.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Publishing video content as requested by you
  • Authenticating your connected accounts (Facebook, YouTube)

Your tokens and credentials are stored securely and are never used for any purpose beyond what you have explicitly authorized.

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data or account credentials with any third parties.

Your data may only be transmitted to official platform APIs (Meta Graph API, YouTube Data API) strictly for the purpose of publishing content on your behalf.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time. Upon a deletion request, all stored tokens and account identifiers associated with your profile will be permanently removed within 30 days.
